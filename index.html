BS fine Sleep Study

This personal research project collects nightly sleep data from Fitbit devices to examine whether BS fine functional garments improve sleep quantity and quality.

Key points

  • Principal investigator: Tomonori Takeuchi (Aarhus University, Denmark)
  • Data collected: sleep stages, skin temperature, SpO2, breathing rate
  • Scope: Single-subject crossover experiment (wear vs non-wear nights)
